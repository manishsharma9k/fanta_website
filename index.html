From Fruit to Fizz

🌿

Step 1 — Sourcing

We partner with local farmers to hand-pick the freshest fruits at peak ripeness, ensuring maximum natural flavor in every batch.

🧪

Step 2 — Extraction

Fruit juices and natural extracts are carefully cold-pressed to preserve their vibrant taste, aroma, and nutritional goodness.

💧

Step 3 — Blending

Our master blenders combine the extracts with purified water and just the right amount of natural sweeteners for that perfect balance.

🫧

Step 4 — Carbonation

The blend is infused with CO₂ under precise pressure, creating those iconic Fanta bubbles that tickle your taste buds.

🥤

Step 5 — Bottling

Sealed fresh in eco-friendly cans and bottles, your Fanta is ready to travel from our factory straight to your hands.
